Girl Gone So Weak

-an originally created poem
First stanza
  1. A curve thats upside down

    2. A smile thats turned into frown
    Tears rolling down her cheeks
    Shes been depressed for a couple of weeks
Second stanza
  1. She likes to cut her wrist

    2. She wished she didnt exist
    Afraid to look at herself in the mirror
    Criticism, such a predictable horror.
Third stanza
  1. She eats her lunch in a toilet seat

    2. And cries her pain between her feet
    Shes not a weirdo, not a freak
    Shes just a girl, gone so weak

"You have been criticizing yourself for years, and it hasnt worked. Try approving of yourself and see what happens.   "
